BuildForYou

About

BuildForYou is a non-profit organization whose mission
is to build and provide functional, quality, and
comfortable housing for impoverished communities. We
believe that every human being has a right to shelter,
and we especially want to bring that luxury to less
fortunate individuals. If you want to support our cause,
you can donate to us here!

Become A Member!
